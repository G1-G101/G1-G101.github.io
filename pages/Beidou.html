Beidou

The Chinese network known as Beidou consists of 30 satellites which purpose is navigation and even messaging. Making it a rival to the GPS that is owned by the U.S., which means that Beidou is independent to the Chinese military use other than using GPS making it a system that is resilient and can be used in time of conflict.
