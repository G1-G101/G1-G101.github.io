Satellite positioning system

Risks

There are many risks of satellite positioning system (S.P.S.), however the most common of them are the private and military force.

1- Private: In today’s modern day and age privacy has become more challenging to protect, especially with phones satellite positioning functions. Which can be used to keep taps on others and all of there activity information without someone’s consent, making is an overlook into others privacy.

2- Military force: If one country military force has an advanced (S.P.S.), they could use it as an advantage to spy or possibly win a war between countries, because the satellite positioning system works as eye for the military force giving them information’s about enemies or possible attacks.
